Conut AI Operations Agent — Executive Brief

Date: February 2026 | Prepared for: Conut Management | Data Period: Aug–Dec 2025

1. Problem Framing

Conut operates 4 branches (Conut, Conut - Tyre, Conut Jnah, Main Street Coffee) selling specialty chimneys, conuts, beverages, and desserts. Operational decisions—product bundling, inventory, staffing, expansion, and category growth—are currently made without systematic data analysis. This project delivers an AI-powered decision-support system that converts transactional data into actionable business intelligence accessible through any messaging platform via OpenClaw.

3. Recommended Actions

  • Immediate: Launch a "Chimney The One + Conut Berry Mix + Mini The Original" combo deal at a 10% bundle discount — the data's highest-lift (28.25x) and highest-confidence (100%) association.
  • Immediate: Rebalance staffing — shift evening excess at Conut - Tyre and Conut to morning/evening shortfalls at Conut Jnah and Main Street Coffee nights.
  • Short-term: Test an afternoon milkshake promotion (2–5 PM) to grow milkshake revenue share from 2.1% toward parity with coffee (3.8%).
  • Short-term: Introduce a coffee loyalty card targeting repeat customers; coffee is the higher-penetration beverage category and the natural repeat-purchase driver.
  • Medium-term: Conduct foot-traffic study near Conut Jnah (score 0.79, low risk) for potential 5th branch — its operational model is the strongest template for replication.
  • Medium-term: Use the demand forecast API to plan inventory ahead of projected growth ramps, especially for Main Street Coffee and Conut Jnah which show the steepest upward trajectories.

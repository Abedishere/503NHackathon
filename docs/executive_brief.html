Conut AI Operations Agent — Executive Brief

Date: February 2026 | Prepared for: Conut Management | Data Period: Aug–Dec 2025

1. Problem Framing

Conut operates 4 branches (Conut, Conut - Tyre, Conut Jnah, Main Street Coffee) selling specialty chimneys, conuts, beverages, and desserts. Operational decisions—product bundling, inventory, staffing, expansion, and category growth—are currently made without systematic data analysis. This project delivers an AI-powered decision-support system that converts transactional data into actionable business intelligence accessible through any messaging platform via OpenClaw.

3. Recommended Actions

  • Immediate: Launch a "Classic Chimney + Conut The One" combo deal at a 10% bundle discount.
  • Immediate: Rebalance staffing — shift 1-2 evening staff from Conut - Tyre and Main Street Coffee to morning/evening at Conut Jnah.
  • Short-term: Test an afternoon milkshake promotion (2–5 PM) to grow the milkshake category.
  • Short-term: Introduce a coffee loyalty card targeting repeat customers to grow the 5% coffee revenue share.
  • Medium-term: Conduct foot-traffic study near Conut Jnah for potential 5th branch location.
  • Medium-term: Increase inventory planning horizon using the demand forecast API ahead of seasonal spikes.
